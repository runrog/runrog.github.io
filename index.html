About

Hello, my name's Roger and I live in San Antonio TX (Go Spurs Go!). I'm a total introvert but I try to use that to my advantage. I feel that it allows me to express my ideas better visually. I love thinking outside of the box and learning new things. I'm currently doing design and front-end development for Rackspace but I also freelance at times. I've been developing since 1999 (back when Space Jam's website was THE website) and started designing digitally a short time after that. I'm pretty well versed in all things front-end (HTML, CSS, LESS, SCSS, Javascript/Jquery etc.).
