About

Hello, my name's Roger and I reside in San Antonio Texas. I'm big on sports (GO SPURS GO!) and all things Texas, especially the BBQ! I'm the kinda guy who lives by faith and see's the positive in any situation. As for work, I currently do design and front-end development for Rackspace but I also freelance at times.

I've been writing font-end code since 1999 and started designing in 2010. All else just fell into place. Designing has been such a natural fit for me and these days I apply myself to learning new areas and strengthening others. I'm pretty well versed in all things front-end (HTML, CSS, LESS, SCSS, Javascript/Jquery).

Development

I mostly contribute to private repositories, so my github account doesn't look as baller as I'd like. As that's the case, I can talk about some of the things I contribute to. I have built a good number of Jquery plugins, things like image galleries, sliders, tabs, graphs, media players etc. Lately I have mostly been working on projects under Rackspace, things like building racker tools and contributing to our internal website and sub apps for rackspace.com. Some of the frameworks I work with there are Angular, Nodejs, Bootstrap and Jquery.
